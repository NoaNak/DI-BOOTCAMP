INGREDIENTS :

  • 3 eggs
  • 150g of sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • 2 spoon of flour
  • 250g of Philadelphia
  • 25 cl heavy cream

Let's go for the preparation of our CheeseCake !

STEP 1:

Preheat the oven to 180Â°C (thermostat 6). Line a 23 cm diameter mold with parchment paper
and previously buttered it. Ideally, the bottom of the mold should be removable, otherwise,
leave the paper to protrude well to easily unmold the cake by pulling on it. Combine biscuits, nutmeg and melted butter.
STEP 2:

Line the bottom of the mold with this mixture, pressing down well with the back of a spoon. Place in the refrigerator.
STEP 3:

Beat the strainer with a whisk until smooth, then add the sugar and the flour and the eggs one by one. Then add the cream and flavor of your choice.
STEP 4:

Pour this preparation over the biscuit crust (if the crust "protrudes" from the cream, remove the excess with a spoon, it may burn).
STEP 5:

Bake for 50-55 minutes until the cheese mixture is firm to the touch.
STEP 6:

Leave to cool, unmold and serve chilled.

Enjoy your meal !
