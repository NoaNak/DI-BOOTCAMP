INGREDIENTS :

  • 150g of flour
  • 85g of sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 85g of butter
  • 1 tablespoon oh salt
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 100g of dark chocolat
  • 1 sachet of vanilla sugarr

Let's go for the preparation of our cookies !

STEP 1:

Cut the chocolate into nuggets.
STEP 2:

Preheat the oven to 180Â°C (thermostat 6). In a salad bowl, put 75 g of butter, the sugar, the whole egg, the vanilla and mix everything with a wooden spoon.
STEP 3:

Gradually add the flour mixed with the baking powder, the salt and the chocolate.
STEP 4:

With a sheet of paper towel, butter a baking sheet and form the cookies on the sheet.
STEP 5:

To form the cookies, use 2 tablespoons and make small heaps spaced from each other; they will grow when cooked.
STEP 6:

Bake for 10 minutes.

Enjoy your meal !
